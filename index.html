About Abhivarta

ABHIVARTA is a celebration of all forms of sports and talent. Each year, the Extravaganza is held for three days for the complete showcase of budding talent of the students from all around and the efforts put it in the months before the fest by our University's students.

Manipal Education Group, a renowned name in the field of education has started its fifth venture – Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ), on an invitation from the Government of Rajasthan. It was established in 2011 and has been growing ever since, with its career-oriented courses across diverse streams and many innovative and professional training programmes.
The college was itself inaugurated by Shri Vasundhara Raje (CM, Rajasthan). Manipal University Jaipur has successfully organized many events in the past and has successfully managed to put itself on the Map of Jaipur and the entire of Rajasthan as the home to emerging talent.Many international conferences like ETMN choose us as a venue. With globally renowned standards of education, we believe in the holistic development of our students to make them ready for the industry they plan to step into. The college hosts three major fests everywhere to acknowledge the budding talent of the students, Oneiros (The Annual Cultural Fest), Abhivarta (The Annual Sports Fest) and Techideate (The Annual Technical Fest). Spread across 121 Acres, the campus is known to be one of the most beautiful colleges in India.
